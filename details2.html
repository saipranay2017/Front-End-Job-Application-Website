Websol Energy System Ltd

Websol Energy Systems Ltd., formerly Websol Energy Systems ltd., manufactures photovoltaic monocrystalline solar cells and modules in India. Websol modules are designed for grid and standalone Solar PV power plants, remote communication and rural electrification under diffused Sunlight. Incorporated in 1990, Websol Energy Systems Ltd. has its registered office located in Kolkata.
