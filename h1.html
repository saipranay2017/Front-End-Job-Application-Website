National Hydroelectric Power Corporation (NHPC)

National Hydroelectric Power Corporation (NHPC) A/S is a Danish manufacturer, seller, installer, and servicer of wind turbines that was founded in 1945. The company operates manufacturing plants in Denmark, Germany, the Netherlands, Taiwan, India, Italy, Romania, the United Kingdom, Spain, Sweden, Norway, Australia, China, Brazil, Poland and the United States, and employs more than 25,000 people globally.As of 2013, it is the largest wind turbine company in the world.
